Hello! My name is Pablo.

I am finishing my undergraduate in Robotics and Digital Systems Engineering at Tec de Monterrey, Campus Guadalajara Mexico. My main interest lies in robustness for autonomous navigation and exploration. I am particularly interested in exploring frameworks for autonomy that can be reliably deployed and adapt to highly dynamic environments.

Throughout my studies, I have sought to develop projects that explore different facets of autonomous systems from control to artificial intelligence. I invite you to checkout my [portfolio](/projects/) for more information on what I have done.
